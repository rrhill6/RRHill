<About Me...>

Building websites started as a hobby for me several years ago with a "little" HTML knowledge and some free time. After many years of teaching high school science, I decided to turn my former hobby into a new career. With the help of many books and lots of online resources; I taught myself HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. To add more to my skillset I enrolled in the code immersion course at Tech Talent South here in Atlanta. My current work uses mostly HTML5 with CSS3. Where additional functionality is needed; JavaScript, Ruby (along with Ruby on Rails) and a little Bootstrap is used on projects.

When I'm not attached to a computer; I enjoy traveling, kayaking and running on trails.
